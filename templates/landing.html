01 — Train

Logging that keeps up with your workout

The screen you use between sets should never slow you down. Fit Logger pre-fills what you lifted last time, so most sets are two taps and done.

  • Last time, right there

    Pick an exercise and the sets you hit last session appear instantly — so you know exactly what to beat.

  • Steppers, not keyboards

    Weight moves in 2.5 kg taps, reps in 1. No number pad, no typos, no fumbling with sweaty hands.

  • Walk away, come back

    A workout stays open until you finish it. Close the tab mid-session and you resume on the exact exercise you left.

  • Every session, kept

    Your history stores each workout grouped by exercise, with notes you wrote at the time.

{# ===================== FUEL ===================== #}

04 — Fuel

Training is half of it

Fit Logger works out what you burn, then turns your chosen goal into grams on a plate — with the food tables to hit them.

  • Calories, properly calculated

    Mifflin-St Jeor for your BMR, scaled by how active you really are — the same maths dietitians use, not a guess.

  • Seven goals, one table

    From a mild cut to a fast bulk, see every target side by side — with unsafe deficits flagged rather than quietly served.

  • Macros in grams

    Protein set to your bodyweight, fat as a share of intake, carbs filling the rest, fibre on top — plus food tables so you know what that looks like.
